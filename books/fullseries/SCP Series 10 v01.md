  ### STRUCTURES

  - A: Everything in this world is governed and confined by structures, whether physical or immaterial.
  - B: Nothing is permanent. There is no structure in this world that will not, one day, return to dust.

  ### CONSTRUCTS

  - A: Everything that is meaningful can be broken down into base elements to be categorized, explained, and made ordinary.
  - B: Nothing is absolute. Even the mundane and well understood can give rise to undiscovered mystery.

  ### PATTERNS

  - A: Everything is accounted for in the grand score of the universe, from its very first motions to its final, dying breath.
  - B: Nothing is inevitable. There will always be an exception to the rule, an outlier that dares to break the cycle.
